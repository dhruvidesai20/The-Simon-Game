  • Start the game by pressing any key.

  • For succeding levels, you'll have to press the whole sequence of buttons i.e. all the buttons that flashed in the previous levels and the button that flashed in the current level. For example, For level 2, one of the buttons will flash. This time you've to press the button flashed in the first level and the button that flashed for the current level.
  • The difficulty level will increase with the increasing levels. Watch and listen carefully and keep in mind the sequence of buttons.
